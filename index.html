Discover the Magic of Indian Handloom Products with Living Quarters

Living Quarters is a one-stop destination for all your Indian handloom product needs. We are a manufacturer, exporter, wholesaler, and retailer, and our vast product range includes handmade bedsheets, Persian carpets, Indian home furniture, women's and men's clothing, jewelry, kitchen and dining, home decor, and much more.

Our business model allows us to offer our products to consumers at prices that are up to 70% lower than the market rate. This is because we source our materials directly from the artisans, eliminating the need for intermediaries and the additional costs that come with them. As a result, we can offer our customers high-quality products at competitive prices.

Shipping Policy

Cost and Freight, or CnF

includes the cost of the product, regional taxes, local transportation, customs clearance at the port of loading, and sea freight. 

Free on Board. FoB

comprises the cost of the product, local taxes, local transportation, and customs clearance at the loading port.

Cost, Insurance, and Freight, or CIF 

includes the cost of the product, local taxes, local transportation, customs clearance at the port of loading, product insurance, and sea freight.

Product cost, local taxes, and local expenses

including labour, packing, and customs fees, if any, are included in the "ex factory" price. 

Transit time

is the length of time it takes a ship to sail from one port to another. 

Length of time

required to prepare the items. This will also take into account the 2 week local transit time from the factory to the port.
